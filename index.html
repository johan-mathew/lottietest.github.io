Life is a domino effect

One small action leads to another

Which slowly builds you up and

occasionally knocks you down!

And so are those budding Ideas!

Don't ever doubt that small single thought that can

trigger an unstoppable chain of events

that gain momentum with increasing force

at the end of which lies something fruitful

or even revolutionary!

so embrace the domino effect and keep on building, until one day

you get to witness the magic of your worthwhile contributions!
